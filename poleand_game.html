Welcome to Poleand

The goal is to eliminate all enemies (purple healthbars)!

You control atomic troops with positive or negative polarity.
Opposites attract and destroy each other. Same polarities ignore each other. You can change your troops' polarity anytime. Each wave increases in difficulty.

Choose from 5 units, each with unique strengths.
Some shoot neutrons (damage only), others fire electrons (which also convert enemy polarity to match).
